How to use this site

Need a half-way meeting point?

With Treff, you can find a half-way point quick and easy. Enter in your address and the emails or phone numbers of the people you want to meet up with and Treff will automatically find a meeting point and send individual directions to everyone. Meanwhile, everyone's Location is kept private.
